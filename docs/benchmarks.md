## Performance Notes

- **DR encrypt is ~1000x faster than DR decrypt** because encrypt has the chain key pre-derived, while decrypt may need to derive through a gap and cache skipped keys.
- **X3DH is the most expensive crypto operation** at ~460 µs, but this only happens once per session establishment.
- **Storage encrypt/decrypt is extremely fast** (~1 µs) — the bottleneck in message loading is not crypto but SQLite I/O and Rust deserialization.
- **Padding is effectively free** for small messages (~0.7 µs) and only becomes measurable at 5 KB+ (~3.8 µs).
